Darts Promotion Demo

How to use:

  1. Click "Test Win Scenario" to simulate a winning promotion
  2. Click "Test Lose Scenario" to simulate a losing promotion
  3. In the game, simply click anywhere on the screen to throw the dart
  4. For win scenario: Dart will always hit bullseye
  5. For lose scenario: Dart will hit random non-bullseye areas
  6. After the animation, a modal will appear with the result
  7. Click "Continue" to return to this demo

Integration Instructions:

To integrate with your promotion system:

  • Set PROMOTION_MODE = true in settings.js
  • Set PROMOTION_RESULT = MODE_PROMOTION_WIN or MODE_PROMOTION_LOSE
  • Or use URL parameters: ?promotion_result=win or ?promotion_result=lose
  • Listen for events: promotion_win_complete or promotion_lose_complete
